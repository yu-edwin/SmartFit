# SmartFit Privacy Policy

_Last updated: [Dec 6, 2025]_

SmartFit ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that helps you manage your wardrobe and create outfits. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SmartFit app ("App").

By using SmartFit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

- Email address  
- Password (stored in hashed form, not as plain text)

### 1.2 Wardrobe & Usage Data

When you use the App, we collect:

- Clothing item images you upload (e.g., photos from your device or camera)
- Item details you enter (such as brand, category, size, color, notes, and outfit combinations)
- Basic interaction data about how you use the App (e.g., adding, editing, or deleting items and outfits)

### 1.3 Device & Technical Information

We may collect limited technical data, such as:

- Device type, operating system version, and App version  
- Crash logs and diagnostic information used to improve stability and performance

We do **not** intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as precise location, payment card details, or government identifiers.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Create and maintain your SmartFit account  
- Store and display your wardrobe items and outfits  
- Provide core App features (filters, search, outfit creation, etc.)  
- Improve and debug the App, including monitoring performance and fixing bugs  
- Communicate with you about important updates or issues related to the App

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers and databases used by SmartFit. We use reasonable technical measures to protect your information, including:

- Encrypted connections (HTTPS) between the App and our backend services  
- Storing passwords using industry-standard hashing techniques

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 4. Sharing of Information

These providers are only allowed to use your data as necessary to provide services to us, and they are not permitted to sell your data.

We do **not** share your information with advertisers or sell your personal data to third parties.
